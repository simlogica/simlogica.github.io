🛠️ <b>How Does Blocker Work?</b>

Blocker intercepts incoming calls and follows a personalized handling strategy based on who is calling.

You define the rules for three contact groups:

⭐ <b>FAVORITES</b>
😃 <b>CONTACTS</b> (People in your Phonebook)
❓ <b>UNKNOWN</b> (Numbers not in your Phonebook)

For each group, choose one of three actions:

🔔 <b>RING</b>: The normal, loud phone ring.
🎵 <b>NOTIFY</b>: Blocker's signature feature! Silences the main ringer but plays a customizable, short sound with vibration. The visual call screen still appears.
🔕 <b>DROP</b>: Sends the call immediately to voicemail without waking up your screen. Total silence.

<i>(All handled calls remain visible in your phone's default Call History. Blocker simply stops the loud ringing for groups you choose to Notify or Drop)</i>.

🌟 Powerful Core Features

• <b>Emergency Bypass</b>: Expecting an urgent call from an unknown number? If the same number calls 3 times within 15 minutes, Blocker will automatically allow it to <b>RING</b>.
• <b>Beat Spam Peacefully</b>: Set <b>UNKNOWN</b> callers to <b>NOTIFY</b>. You get a subtle, pleasant "ping" instead of a loud disruption. Pick up if it's convenient or let it slide.
