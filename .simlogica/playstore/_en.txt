== Blocker Call Controller
== Blocker is an innovative way to handle mobile phone calls.

<b>Blocker</b> is an innovative way to handle mobile phone calls.

Loud rings are a thing from the past!

Blocker aims for a modern, non-disturbing, pleasant, and clever approach to handling incoming mobile phone calls with its <b>NOTIFY</b> feature.

Blocker replaces the old traditional loud-ringer of mobile phone calls with a convenient and pleasant short sound and vibration, alongside the call notification on the screen, for each of your contact's groups. Whenever you want.

<b>How does it work?</b>

Selectively set incoming phone calls handling based on these groups:
⇒ <b>Favorites</b>
⇒ <b>Contacts</b>
⇒ <b>Unknown numbers</b>

Let them:
⇒ <b>RING</b> – Old intrusive way.
⇒ <b>NOTIFY</b> – Blocker new way!!
⇒ <b>DROP immediately</b> – "I am not in the mood to talk" option.

All the calls that Blocker handles are shown in your <b>Phone Call App's</b> history. Blocker just gets in to prevent the call to <b>RING</b> for those groups you chose to <b>NOTIFY</b> or <b>DROP</b>. Your call app will still show all notifications from received calls that you did not pick up.

⇒ Study

⇒ Just don't be bothered
⇒ Meditate
⇒ Be pleasantly alerted of an incoming call during a meeting
⇒ Get a rest

You name it. Conveniently set the adequate level of interference with Blocker call handling options for each occasion.

Blocker is an essential app for virtually anybody on the planet that has an Android phone.

Blocker <b>NOTIFY</b> feature is also amazingly helpful when you are in an app's call, and you receive a mobile phone call. Usually your Bluetooth headset will go nuts with the incoming call. But not anymore if the incoming call is held by Blocker's <b>NOTIFY</b>. Blocker short sound will be fast played, and you won't get sound blocked by the incoming call in your Bluetooth headset.

<b>What about anti-spamming?</b> Spammers are getting more and more aggressive around the world, and antispammers are just failing to deal with that. Either allowing false positives to ring or filtering legitime callers.

With the Blocker's new approach to phone calls, this is not an issue any more if you set <b>UNKNOWN</b> calls to the <b>NOTIFY</b> option. You get peace of mind with Blocker's pleasant one-time short sound. No loud rings to bother you.

Pick up the phone only when it is convenient, or let Spam calls just fill your recent call log. They will be there just in case you need to occasionally get back to any of them.

Also, we have grouped every call-related setting available in your phone within an organized fast to access menu. You don't need to fight the phone's complex default Settings app to set your call-related preferences.

Blocker is also lightweight, blazing fast and does not collect or share your data. It is built with a <b>user-privacy-first</b> mindset design, and it has a tiny footprint.

Experience a new healthier way to deal with phone calls. A new way in which you are peacefully in control.
