    Blocker App

    Welcome to SimLogica.com, the home of Blocker Call Controller App.

    Blocker transforms the traditional loud ring into pleasant short sound notifications with its NOTIFY feature.

    Bringing control and peace of mind to us over incoming mobile phone calls. Spam will never be an issue any more.

    Blocker Call Controller

    Get it on Google Play

    What can Blocker do for you?

    By default, Blocker will set UNKNOWN contacts to the NOTIFY feature. So instead of the loud ring from the past, a customizable pleasant sound and vibration will take place, alongside the call notification on the screen.

    Loud rings are a thing from the past. Blocker brings an innovative way to deal with mobile phone calls with its NOTIFY feature.

    That means we can enjoy a more modern, non-disturbing, pleasant, and clever approach to handling incoming mobile phone calls.

    Experience the Blocker way for a week and feel the difference!

    How does Blocker work?

    Blocker gets in the way of every incoming mobile phone call and follows the handling strategy you define for each contact group:

    FAVORITES
    CONTACTS – other people in your Phone Book
    UNKNOWN – people not in your Phone Book

    Let them:

    RING
    NOTIFY – Blocker new way with customizable short sounds and vibration.
    DROP immediately.

    NOTIFY silences the phone ringer but plays a customizable short sound and vibration and also shows the traditional phone visual notification on the screen.

    DROP sends calls directly to voicemail without lighting up the screen. It's a total silence option when needed.

    There is also an option to allow a third repeated call from the same phone number within 15 minutes to RING anyway.

    Blocker Presets

    By default, we set UNKNOWN callers to NOTIFY and the other groups to RING.

    But we really encourage everyone to try our Focus Mode, that sets both CONTACTS and UNKNOWN callers to NOTIFY. That is invaluable when you want to focus on something important or is attending a meeting.

    Usually the NOTIFY option produces a short sound and vibration that is enough to bring our attention most of the time.

    All the calls that Blocker handles are shown in your Phone Call App's history. Blocker just gets in to prevent the call to RING for those groups set to NOTIFY and DROP. Respecting your privacy, not sharing your data.

    Other interesting use cases

    Simultaneous calls

    Blocker Notify feature is also amazingly helpful when you are in any other app's call, and you receive a mobile phone call.

    Usually the Bluetooth headset will prioritize the mobile incoming phone call. But not any more if the incoming call is held by Blocker's NOTIFY feature.

    Blocker short sound will be fast played, and you won't get sound blocked by the incoming call in your Bluetooth headset.

    Antispam

    What about anti-spamming?

    Spammers are getting more and more aggressive around the world, and antispammers are just failing to deal with that. They either filter legit calls or let spam calls to ring.

    With Blocker default, UNKNOWN callers in NOTIFY, we don't get ringed by them any more. That is the whole point of Blocker, don't get ringed by unknown people.

    Urgent callers will try again and usually fall in the 15-minute repeated call rule.

    So is Blocker an Antispam app? We think it is even better. Don't take our word for granted. Try it for yourself.

    Just bear in mind that if your Phone App has an active built-in antispam, it may still block calls that Blocker would have allowed.
