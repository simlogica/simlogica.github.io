### What can Blocker do for you?

Loud phone rings are a thing from the past. Blocker brings an innovative way to deal with mobile phone calls with its **NOTIFY** feature.

The **NOTIFY** feature substitutes the loud ring with a short customizable sound alert and vibration, alongside the call notification on the screen, for each of your contact's groups.

That means that we can enjoy a more modern, non-disturbing, pleasant, and clever approach to handling incoming mobile phone calls whenever we want.

### How does Blocker work?

Blocker gets in the way of every mobile phone call and follows the strategy defined for each contact group:

:material-star: **Favorites**<br>
:material-star: Other **Contacts** in your Phone Book<br>
:material-star: **Unknown** numbers

Let them:

:material-star: **RING** – the old loud and intrusive way.<br>
:material-star: **NOTIFY** – Blocker new way with customizable short sounds, volumes, and vibration.<br>
:material-star: **DROP** immediately – the total silence option.

**NOTIFY** silences the phone ringer but plays a customizable short sound and vibration and also shows the traditional phone visual notification on the screen.

**DROP** sends calls directly to voicemail without lighting up the screen. It's a total silence option when needed.

There is the option to allow third repeated calls within 15 minutes to **RING** anyway. That is mostly enough to let you pick up something really urgent.

### Blocker recommended defaults

By default, we just set **Unknown** callers to **NOTIFY**. But we really encourage everyone to try our **Focus Mode**, that sets both **Contacts** and **Unknown** callers to **NOTIFY**.

All the calls that Blocker handles are shown in the **Phone Call App's** history for you to review them if necessary. Blocker just gets in to prevent the call to **RING** for those groups set to **NOTIFY** and **DROP**.

### Other use cases

#### Simultaneous calls

Blocker Notify feature is also amazingly helpful when you are in an app's call, and you receive a mobile phone call.

Usually the Bluetooth headset will go nuts with the incoming call.

But not any more if the incoming call is held by Blocker's **NOTIFY** feature. Blocker short sound will be fast played, and you won't get sound blocked by the incoming call in your Bluetooth headset.

#### Antispam

What about anti-spamming?

Spammers are getting more and more aggressive around the world, and antispammers are just failing to deal with that. Set **Unknown** callers to **NOTIFY** and they can't bother any more.

That is why we consider Block also an Antispam app, but not in the traditional sense. It is not a filter that tries to guess if an incoming call is a spam or not, but a different (and we think it is more clever) approach to the issue.

Bear in mind that if your Phone App has a built-in antispam, it may still block calls that Blocker would have allowed.
